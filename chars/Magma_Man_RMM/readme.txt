Magma Man (マグママン Maguma Man) is a Robot Master from Mega Man 9 modeled after an incinerator that Dr. Light created to oversee safety operations and manage a geothermal power plant. He is able to withstand incredibly high temperatures and loves to visit hot spas.

The lavafalls in his stage have the same effect as the laser beams from Quick Man's stage in Mega Man 2, the ignited oil from Flame Man's stage in Mega Man 6, and the fire beams from Turbo Man's stage in Mega Man 7, which all cause Mega Man to die instantly.

RMM features Power Sets for each char, which can be changed by doing the right command.


D,D, any attack button	Power Sets	
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
	Switch Power to power sets 1 through 6:

	01 (lk) = Magma Bazooka (buttons controls the angle)
	02 (mk) = Living Flame (buttons controls the distance)
	03 (hk) = Heat Tornado* (buttons controls the angle)
	04 (lp) = Magma Mortar (buttons controls the number of projectiles)
	05 (mp) = F.R.I.E.N.D.S (buttons changes the minion)
	06 (hp) = Fire Shield (buttons controls the duration)

* can be done on air too

D,F, K	Use Weapon
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
	Uses the weapon from the active Power Set

QCF+P		Flame Sword - 1st strike
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
	Dashes forward hitting with his Flame Sword, in “rekka” style (can be cancelled into itself)

	-> QCF+P		Flame Sword - 2st strike
	->> QCF+P		Flame Sword - 3rd strike

Flame Sword can be either canceled from itself upon each hit or at the end of animation



F,D,DF+P	 Blazing Upper
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
	Your trivial uppercut attack :)
