Metalman Move List
----------

QCF+P: Throw Metal Blade
F,D,DF+P: Blade Uppercut
D,DB,B+P: Spike Ball

D, D, any button: Change Weapon
	LP: Buzzsaw
	MP: Mini Dragon attack
	HP: Quickman
	LK: Chainsaw
	MK: Dust Explosion
	HK: Blade Dive
QCF+K: Use Weapon

D,DF,F+PP: Metal Blade Barrage (Can be done in the Air too)
D,DB,B+PP: Bladed Armed Fury (Can be done in the Air too)
D,DF,F+KK: Mega Dragon
QCB+PP:  Hyper Spiked Ball  (In Air Only)
