###########|NOTES|###########

Mugen 1.0+ only.
This is intented to be a CPU BOSS char.
It can't jump and just have a weak point - its eyes. You can hit its helmet or legs, but it receives no damage.
Thanx to that, its hard to make combos on him, except for its eyes.
It can't be grabbed or put into custom states.
